- Back button, Search This Area button, Center button

Bottom Section (30-45 Percent):
- Dark bottom sheet
- Hostel name, weather, distance
- Address, rating
- Three-image gallery
- View Details button

- Border: 4 px white

- Label: hostel name and distance

--------------------

Center Button:
- Circular location icon
